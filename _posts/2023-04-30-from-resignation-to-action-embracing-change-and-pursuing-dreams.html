There has been some change since I wrote my last publication. Let’s say that I finaly put my words to action: I gave my resignation to my employer.

And let me tell you something, it was the best decision EVER! I took a week to relax and put some order in my head. I had to decide what to do next. I did not have the luxury of taking my time. I was a little panicked at first and was second-guessing my decision.

I have taken a leap of faith and hit up some shop owners. Maybe, my two years of mechanical engineering studies could land me a job. Half an hour later, I found myself sitting in a job interview, and guess what? I totally nailed it!

I am now working as a machinist in a family-owned old-school machine shop. The job I was aiming for ten years ago.

I’m still pinching myself. It only took me ten days to make the switch! Goodbye soul-sucking robotic job, hello new gig!

On top of that, the shop is less than a mile from my home. I mean, seriously, what were the chances of that? I can roll out of bed and be there in five minutes flat. It’s insane!

I haven’t given up on my cybersecurity dream. In fact, I’m feeling super confident about the future. I just had to ditch the stuff that was draining my ambition.

Stay tuned, my friend! I’ve been doing serious work over the past couple of days. I am working on a master plan, a publication plan/calendar, to be exact. I’m getting organized, and I am, more than ever, making moves toward my ultimate dream.

Thanks for reading!
